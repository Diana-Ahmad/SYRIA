Popular Cities in Syria

Damascus, the capital of Syria, is one of the oldest continuously inhabited cities in the world. Known for its rich history, ancient landmarks like the Umayyad Mosque, and its vibrant cultural heritage, it offers a blend of tradition and modernity.

Lattakia is a coastal city in western Syria, known for its beautiful beaches, Mediterranean climate, and historical sites. It’s the perfect blend of nature and history, offering visitors scenic views, relaxing resorts, and ancient ruins like the Crusader Castle.

Homs, known as the "Heart of Syria," is a city rich in both ancient history and modern development. It is home to the famous Krak des Chevaliers, a UNESCO World Heritage site, and offers a glimpse into Syria’s cultural and historical legacy.
